Online Voting Rules

  • Each registered voter can cast only one vote per election.
  • Voters must verify their identity using a valid Voter ID and Aadhar number.
  • Votes must be cast within the official election time window.
  • Once submitted, a vote cannot be modified or withdrawn.
  • Only verified candidates are listed on the voting page.
  • Any attempt to manipulate or tamper with the system will result in disqualification.
  • Voter anonymity and data security are strictly enforced.
  • The use of bots, scripts, or third-party tools is strictly prohibited.
  • Disputes will be resolved solely by the election authority’s final decision.
  • Participation indicates acceptance of all terms and rules mentioned above.
